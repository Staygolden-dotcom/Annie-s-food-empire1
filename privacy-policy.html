Privacy Policy

Last Updated: may 10, 2025

Welcome to Annie's Food Empire! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery/billing address
  • Payment details (processed securely via third-party payment processors)
  • Order history

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type & device information
  • IP address
  • Cookies & tracking data (for analytics and site improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To fulfill and deliver your food orders.
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries, feedback, or complaints.
  • Marketing: To send promotional offers, newsletters, or updates (you can opt out anytime).
  • Improvements: To analyze website traffic and enhance user experience.
  • Security: To prevent fraud and unauthorized access.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Delivery Partners: To fulfill your orders.
  • Payment Processors: To securely complete transactions.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law (e.g., court orders, fraud investigations).

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (e.g., saved cart items).
  • Analyze website performance via tools like Google Analytics.
  • Deliver targeted ads (you can disable cookies in your browser settings).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • SSL encryption for secure transactions.
  • Secure payment gateways (we do not store credit card details).
  • Limited employee access to personal data.

However, no online system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and notify us immediately of any suspicious activity.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails (via the "Unsubscribe" link).
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@anniesfoodempire.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external sites (e.g., delivery partners, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices, so review their policies separately.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect children's data. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on our website with the effective date.
